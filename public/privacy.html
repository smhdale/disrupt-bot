Disrupt17 Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection & Use

  • Disrupt17 only collects your name and profile picture for use in personalised messages between you and the Disrupt17 Facebook Messenger chat bot.
  • Your privacy is respected. Data collected is only stored by Disrupt17, and is not shared with or sent to any third parties.
  • Data collected is stored on a secure server with reasonable safeguards in place to prevent unauthorised acccess, however 100% data safety cannot be guaranteed.
  • Messages sent to the Disrupt17 Facebook Messenger chat bot are not stored by Disrupt17, but are passed through Facebook and Wit.ai APIs. Privacy policies for these services can be found here:

    • Wit.ai Privacy Policy
